Thamizhi originates from Tamil culture and values, but its vision is global. Built by Tamil people, designed for the world.

## Tamil Roots, Universal Values

Tamil civilization has a deep tradition of truth-seeking and community service:
- **Thirukkural** — universal ethics for all humanity
- **Sangam literature** — community storytelling across generations
- **Justice movements** — fighting for equality and dignity
- **Language preservation** — maintaining identity while embracing the world

These values inform Thamizhi's mission: **truth, community, and access for everyone**.
