Welcome to Thamizhi. This guide will help you get started with the platform, whether you're reading news, submitting stories, or contributing to verification.

## Accessing Thamizhi

### Web Application
- Visit **thamizhi.app** in any modern browser
- No installation required
- Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile

### Mobile Application
- Download from your device's app store
- Available for iOS and Android
- Same features as web version

## Creating an Account

1. **Visit the signup page**
   - Click "Sign Up" on the homepage
   - Choose your preferred method (email, phone, or social login)

2. **Verify your identity**
   - Complete the simple verification process
   - This helps maintain community trust

3. **Set up your profile**
   - Choose a display name
   - Select your interests and location
   - Write a brief bio (optional)

## Your First Experience

### Reading News
- Browse the **Home** feed for latest stories
- Use **Categories** to filter by topic
- Search for specific keywords or locations
- Save articles for later reading

### Understanding Verification
Every story on Thamizhi shows its verification status:
- **AI Verified**: Processed by our AI pipeline
- **Community Verified**: Reviewed by community members
- **Expert Verified**: Validated by subject matter experts
- **Pending**: Awaiting verification

### Engaging with Content
- **Upvote/Downvote**: Help surface quality content
- **Comment**: Add context or ask questions
- **Share**: Spread important stories
- **Report**: Flag inaccurate or harmful content

## Next Steps

Once you're comfortable with the basics:
- Learn about [Citizen Submission](/user-guide/citizen-submission) to contribute your own stories
- Explore [Verification Layers](/user-guide/verification) to understand how quality is maintained
- Discover [Reputation System](/user-guide/reputation) to build trust in the community
