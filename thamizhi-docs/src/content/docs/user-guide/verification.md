Verification is the cornerstone of trust in Thamizhi. Our multi-layer system combines AI efficiency with human judgment to ensure accuracy.

## The Three-Layer System

### Layer 1: AI Pre-screening
**Automatic processing within minutes**

The AI pipeline performs initial verification:
- **Content analysis**: Checks for factual consistency
- **Duplicate detection**: Identifies similar stories
- **Source verification**: Validates referenced information
- **Bias detection**: Flags potential one-sided reporting
- **Quality scoring**: Rates overall reliability

**What AI checks:**
- Grammar and clarity
- Logical consistency
- Factual claims against known databases
- Image authenticity (reverse search)
- Link validity

**Limitations:**
- Cannot verify eyewitness accounts
- May miss cultural context
- Struggles with satire or irony
- Needs human judgment for complex issues

### Layer 2: Community Review
**24-48 hours of peer evaluation**

Community members review submissions:
- **Accuracy voting**: Upvote/downvote on factual claims
- **Comment feedback**: Questions and suggestions
- **Source verification**: Community validates references
- **Context addition**: Members add background information
- **Bias identification**: Community flags potential issues

**Community review process:**
1. Submission appears in review queue
2. Qualified reviewers examine content
3. They vote on accuracy (1-5 scale)
4. Comments provide detailed feedback
5. Author can respond and revise
6. Final community vote determines outcome

**Reviewer qualifications:**
- Account age > 30 days
- Reputation score > 70
- Active participation history
- No recent violations

### Layer 3: Expert Validation
**Specialized review for complex topics**

Subject matter experts verify:
- **Technical accuracy**: For scientific or technical claims
- **Legal compliance**: For legal or regulatory content
- **Medical accuracy**: For health-related information
- **Historical facts**: For historical claims
- **Cultural context**: For cultural or social topics

**Expert network:**
- Verified professionals in various fields
- Academic researchers
- Industry specialists
- Community leaders with domain expertise

**When expert validation is triggered:**
- High-impact stories
- Technical or scientific claims
- Legal or medical content
- Controversial topics
- Community request

## Verification Status

Every story shows its verification level:

| Status | Meaning | Trust Level |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 🟡 Pending | Awaiting review | Low |
| 🔵 AI Verified | Passed AI screening | Medium |
| 🟢 Community Verified | Community approved | High |
| 🟣 Expert Verified | Expert validated | Very High |
| 🔴 Disputed | Accuracy questioned | Variable |

## The Verification Timeline

```
Submission → AI Screening (minutes) → Community Review (24-48h) → Publication
                                    ↓
                              Expert Validation (if needed)
                                    ↓
                              Final Verification
```

## Factors Affecting Verification Speed

**Faster verification:**
- Clear, well-structured submissions
- Multiple credible sources
- Author with good reputation
- Straightforward factual content
- No controversial claims

**Slower verification:**
- Complex or technical content
- Controversial or sensitive topics
- Author with limited history
- Need for expert validation
- Community disagreement

## After Verification

### Verified Stories
- Appear in main feed with verification badge
- Higher visibility in search results
- Contribute to author's reputation
- Eligible for platform promotion

### Disputed Stories
- Flagged with dispute notice
- Author can appeal or revise
- Community discussion encouraged
- May be removed if unresolvable

## Improving Verification Outcomes

**For authors:**
- Provide multiple sources
- Include context and background
- Respond to community feedback
- Build reputation over time
- Be transparent about limitations

**For reviewers:**
- Focus on facts, not opinions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Vote consistently
- Report genuine issues
- Help authors improve

## Verification Metrics

Track your verification performance:
- **Approval rate**: Percentage of submissions verified
- **Average review time**: How quickly your content is reviewed
- **Community feedback score**: Average rating from reviewers
- **Expert validation rate**: How often expert review is needed

## Common Verification Issues

**Q: Why was my submission rejected?**
A: Check the feedback for specific reasons. Common issues include insufficient sources, factual errors, or bias.

**Q: Can I appeal a verification decision?**
A: Yes, you can appeal through the dispute process. Provide additional evidence or context.

**Q: How do I become a qualified reviewer?**
A: Build your reputation through accurate submissions and helpful community engagement.

**Q: What if I disagree with community verification?**
A: You can flag the story for expert review or provide additional evidence in comments.
