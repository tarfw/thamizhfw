The reputation system measures your trustworthiness in the Thamizhi community. Higher reputation gives you more influence and privileges.

## How Reputation Works

Reputation is a score from 0-100 that reflects your community standing. It's calculated based on your actions and how others respond to them.

### Earning Reputation

**Positive actions that increase reputation:**
- **Accurate submissions**: +5 to +15 points per verified story
- **Helpful reviews**: +2 to +5 points per quality review
- **Community engagement**: +1 to +3 points per meaningful comment
- **Mentoring**: +3 to +7 points for helping new members
- **Reporting**: +2 to +4 points for accurate issue reports

**Factors affecting reputation gains:**
- **Consistency**: Regular positive contributions build faster
- **Quality**: High-impact stories earn more
- **Community response**: Upvotes and positive feedback help
- **Time**: Reputation builds gradually

### Losing Reputation

**Actions that decrease reputation:**
- **Rejected submissions**: -3 to -10 points per rejection
- **Poor reviews**: -2 to -5 points for inaccurate reviews
- **Community violations**: -5 to -20 points per violation
- **Inactivity**: Slow decrease over time without participation
- **Disputes**: -2 to -8 points for unresolved disputes

## Reputation Calculation

Your reputation score is calculated using this formula:

```
Base Score = (Accurate Submissions × 5) + (Helpful Reviews × 3) + (Community Engagement × 1)
Penalty Score = (Rejections × 5) + (Violations × 10) + (Disputes × 3)
Final Score = Base Score - Penalty Score
```

**Modifiers:**
- **Consistency bonus**: +10% for daily participation
- **Quality bonus**: +15% for high-impact stories
- **Time decay**: -5% per month of inactivity
- **Community trust**: ±10% based on community feedback

## Common Reputation Questions

**Q: How often is reputation updated?**
A: Reputation is calculated in real-time as actions occur, with daily batch processing for complex calculations.

**Q: Can reputation be reset?**
A: Reputation cannot be reset, but it can be rebuilt through consistent positive contributions.

**Q: What happens if my reputation drops too low?**
A: Low reputation may limit certain privileges, but you can always rebuild through positive engagement.

**Q: How do I recover from reputation loss?**
A: Focus on consistent, high-quality contributions. Time and positive actions will restore your score.
