## Overview

The Blood Donor Registry connects donors with those in need across the community. It is the most fully developed community tool in the platform, with five integrated sections.

## Home Tab

- Live banner showing critical requests in your region
- Community stats — active donors, units donated, lives saved
- AI predictive match insight for anticipated shortages
- Quick actions: donate, request, find nearby donors
- Top urgent requests previewed on the dashboard
- Personal impact statistics for each donor

## Requests Tab

Filter requests by urgency — All, Critical, Urgent, or Routine. Each request card displays:

- Blood group and units needed
- Hospital and patient information
- Urgency badge with visual emphasis for critical cases
- Distance and estimated travel time
- AI match score showing how well you match the request
- Respond button to offer donation

## My Card — Donor Profile

A virtual donor identity card for every registered donor:

- Blood group, name, location
- Last donation date and eligibility status (90-day rule)
- Donation streak tracking
- Availability toggle for emergency alerts
- Health profile — weight, age, recent illness, haemoglobin
- Achievement badges — Bronze, Rapid, Verified

## AI Assist

A conversational assistant for blood donation queries:

- Pre-set suggestion chips — eligibility, camps, shortage predictions, diet tips
- Contextual AI responses tailored to the donor
- 7-day demand forecast chart by blood group
- Guidance on donation preparation and recovery

## Camps Tab

- Upcoming blood donation camps with RSVP
- Donation history timeline
- Downloadable donation certificate
- Camp location and schedule information
