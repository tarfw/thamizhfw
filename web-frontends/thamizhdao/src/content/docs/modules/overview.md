## Community Tools

Alongside the governance modules, the app includes 16 community-driven tools for grassroots organizing and mutual aid:

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Blood Donor Registry** | Complete blood donor network with emergency requests, donor cards, camps, and AI matching |
| **Organ Donation Pledge** | Pledge to donate organs and connect with registries |
| **Tree Plantation Drive** | Organize drives, track sapling survival, assign caretakers |
| **Disaster Relief Volunteers** | Mobilize volunteers for floods and cyclones |
| **Food Donation & Annadanam** | Connect surplus food to kitchens and orphanages |
| **Education Support & Tuition** | Free tuition, scholarships, books, and teacher-learner pairing |
| **Elder & Disability Care** | Home visits, medicine delivery, pension help, companionship |
| **Lake & Water Body Restoration** | Map and revive eris, ponds, and wells |
| **Waste Management & Cleanup** | Ward cleanup drives, segregation training, composting |
| **Animal Welfare & Strays** | Stray care, sterilization, rescue coordination |
| **Skill Training & Livelihood** | Tailoring, electrical, plumbing, digital workshops |
| **Women's Collective** | Self-help groups, domestic violence legal aid, microenterprise |
| **Youth Clubs & Sports** | Sports leagues, youth clubs, mentorship, anti-addiction programs |
| **Reading Circles & Libraries** | Free libraries, Tamil reading circles, travelling book carts |
| **Health Camps & Clinics** | Free check-up camps, eye and dental clinics |
| **Mental Health Support** | Peer support groups, suicide prevention helpline, counselling |
