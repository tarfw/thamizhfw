**தமிழ் DAO** is a decentralized governance operating system connecting Tamil Nadu, Eelam, and diaspora communities through transparent civic infrastructure, AI-assisted accountability, and participatory decision-making at every level.

**சாதி ஒழிப்பு · தமிழர் உரிமை · சமத்துவ பொருளாதாரம் · மக்களாட்சி**

Annihilation of caste · Tamil rights · Socialist economy · People's democracy

## Mobile App Platform

தமிழ் DAO is available as a mobile app for participating in your constituency, connecting with the community, and accessing governance tools.

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Spaces** | Browse Tamil Nadu and Eelam constituencies, pin your spaces, and join community discussions |
| **Feed** | Location-based social feed powered by community conversations |
| **Chats** | Private and group messaging with community members |
| **Community** | View active members in Tamil Nadu and Eelam communities |
| **Profile** | Your public identity with posts, replies, media, and likes |
| **Hub** | Constituency dashboard with 33 governance and community tools |
| **Blood Donor Registry** | Complete blood donor network with emergency requests, donor cards, and AI matching |

All features are accessible after signing in with your Bluesky account.

## Core Philosophy

- **Tamil National Dignity** — Tamil language, land, culture, history, political rights, and collective survival must be protected.
- **Oppressed First** — athithamizhar, workers, farmers, fisherfolk, women, trans people, refugees, disabled, landless, plantation workers, minorities, displaced Tamils — central, not decorative.
- **Casteless Democracy** — The system must fight caste power, social inequality, economic exploitation, and knowledge monopoly.
- **Decentralized Power** — No single leader, party, caste group, company, bureaucracy, or state authority should fully control public life.
- **AI as Servant** — AI should help people understand, organize, audit, and decide. AI should never become a ruler.
