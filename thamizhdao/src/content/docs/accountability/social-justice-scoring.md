## Scoring Metrics

| Score | What It Measures |
|-------|-----------------|
| Caste Equality Score | Discrimination, access, and representation across caste lines |
| Gender Safety Score | Women's safety, participation, and representation |
| Education Access Score | School enrollment, quality, segregation, completion rates |
| Health Access Score | Hospital access, maternal health, nutrition, equity |
| Water Justice Score | Water access equity across colonies, wards, and communities |
| Land Justice Score | Landlessness, land ownership patterns, housing security |
| Budget Fairness Score | Whether public spending reaches historically excluded communities |
| Language Rights Score | Tamil language access in education, administration, judiciary, media |
| Public Participation Score | Voter turnout, assembly attendance, proposal submission |
| Corruption Risk Score | Tender anomalies, contract concentration, complaint patterns |
| Rep Accountability Score | Promise completion, attendance, fund utilization, public satisfaction |
